Hot Sneaks

A 2D graphics and UI coffeescript library.

Examples

  hotsneaks ->
    image "http://i.imgur.com/CbMq1.png"
    ellipse 'center-x': 10, 'center-y': 20, 'radius-x': 30, 'radius-y': 40
    title "Now this is programming!*"
    fineprint "*it's totally not magic"
